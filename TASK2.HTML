Arjit Singh

Arijit Singh, born on April 25, 1987, is an Indian playback singer and music producer who has achieved significant success in the Indian music industry. He began his career by participating in the reality show Fame Gurukul" in 2005 and later assisted music director Pritam. Arijit gained widespread recognition for his soulful voice and romantic renditions, particularly with songs like "Tum Hi Ho" and "Chahun Main Ya Naa".
